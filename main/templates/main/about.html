About Our College

Jayawantrao Sawant College of Engineering (JSCOE), established in 2004 under JSPM, Pune, is one of Maharashtra’s premier engineering institutions. The college is affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University and approved by AICTE, New Delhi. It aims to provide world-class education with a strong emphasis on research, innovation, and industry-oriented learning. JSCOE fosters an ecosystem where students evolve as skilled engineers, critical thinkers, and responsible citizens.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution that provides high-quality technical education and nurtures innovation, research, and entrepreneurship, producing socially responsible and industry-ready professionals.

Our Mission

To create a transformative learning environment that blends academic excellence with practical exposure, empowering students to meet the challenges of a rapidly changing technological world. The college emphasizes value-based education, leadership development, and lifelong learning.

Campus & Facilities

The JSCOE campus is equipped with modern laboratories, digital classrooms, innovation centers, and incubation hubs. The institute also provides hostel facilities, libraries, sports complexes, and research labs — ensuring a complete learning experience that blends academics and extracurricular growth.
