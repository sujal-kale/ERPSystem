Department Of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering

About the Department

Vision

To be a globally recognized center of excellence in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering education and research, fostering innovation and entrepreneurship.

Mission

To impart quality education, promote research in emerging technologies, and develop skilled professionals for industry and society.

The Department of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering at JSCOE offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs with a focus on cutting-edge areas like VLSI, Wireless Communications, Signal Processing, and IoT.
